06 — Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before talking to sales.

What is Kodex?

Kodex is a B2B SaaS company that builds scalable business software — ERP, fleet management, payroll, and fitness management products — designed to adapt to how each business actually operates.

Where is Kodex based?

Kodex is headquartered in Sri Lanka and serves clients globally. Our products are billed in USD at the daily bank rate in LKR for local customers.

How long does implementation take?

Most BusinessX deployments go live in 2–4 weeks. Custom ERP and complex multi-branch rollouts typically take 6–12 weeks depending on integrations.

Can Kodex integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. The Enterprise plan includes API access and custom integrations with accounting platforms, payment gateways, HR systems, and any third-party app via REST APIs.

Do you offer custom development?

Absolutely. Beyond the standard suite, we engineer custom ERP modules and bespoke business software tailored to your exact operational workflows.
